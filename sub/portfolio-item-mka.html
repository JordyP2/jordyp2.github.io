A journey through custom tracks

Mario Kart Archives is a mod for Mario Kart Wii, also known as a custom track distribution, which archives all custom tracks ever made for the game into one downloadable package.

Running on a code extension for Mario Kart Wii called LE-CODE, Mario Kart Archives modifies the game to add more cups than traditionally through coding. containing additional playable race tracks the player can race through in Grand Prix, Time Trials or traditional VS races which rules are also customizable.

Mario Kart Archives gets updated regularly with more tracks released in order of release by the community, also providing them with fitting soundtracks.

A legal copy of Mario Kart Wii and original hardware is required to use this mod
